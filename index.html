About Us

Crocodile Strength Gym is a dedicated strength training facility proudly located in Osizweni (KwaBob Shopping Center), a township in Newcastle, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa.

We are committed to helping our community build raw power, endurance, and confidence through top-quality equipment, structured workouts, and affordable memberships.

Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned lifter, join us to unlock your full potential – one rep at a time.
