About Us

Established in 2025, Crocodile Strength Gym is a proud local fitness facility dedicated to serving the Osizweni community and surrounding areas in Newcastle.

We provide a welcoming and fully equipped environment for all fitness goals — whether you're focused on cardio, strength training, bodybuilding, or general health improvement. Our gym is designed to support members of all levels, from beginners taking their first steps to experienced athletes pushing their limits.

Conveniently located in KwaBob Shopping Center, Shop No C5 — right near Osizweni 3 Clinic — we're easy to find and always ready to welcome you.

Can't find us? Feel free to contact us via WhatsApp or give us a call — we're here to help.

Meet The Founder

Siphephelo Sphiwe Ngwenya

Founder of Crocodile Strength Gym, Siphephelo Sphiwe Ngwenya built this fitness haven from the ground up with passion, determination, and an unwavering belief in the transformative power of strength training.

Starting with a clear vision to create an accessible, no-nonsense gym for the local community, Sphiwe turned ambition into reality through hard work and resilience. Today, Crocodile Strength stands as a testament to what dedication can achieve — a thriving space where people of all backgrounds come together to become stronger, healthier, and more confident.

"I built this gym because I believe everyone deserves a place to grow stronger — not just physically, but mentally and emotionally too. Crocodile Strength is more than a gym; it's a community built on grit, support, and real progress."
